About

I believe that I'm meant for a career in website development. At 8 years old, I designed a personal homepage on MS Paint. At 10, I used a website builder to create my first website. By my teens I dabbled in HTML and graphic design.

As a Computer Software Development graduate, I enjoy creating gorgeous website applications with fantastic UI/UX. My applications are created with consideration for design elements such as site flow, brand colors, contrast, and typography.

For my brain, I love solving puzzles, especially those in video games and escape rooms. For my body, I enjoy singing, dancing, and trail hiking. I taught myself video editing since I was 11 years old. And of course, I am passionate about graphic design.

You can view my designs on my Behance profile, where you can also view a couple of my videos. My most recent video editing work is on the Wilson Analytical YouTube channel.
